# 🟦 BaseBlobs — MVP Foundation v1

## Core Concept
BaseBlobs is a cozy mobile-first collectible web app inspired by Tamagotchi, idle games, and Web3 culture.

Players own cute blob creatures born from Base network energy. They send them on expeditions, collect Cubes, level up, unlock cards, evolve blobs, and build their collection.

The goal is to create a relaxing, stylish, highly replayable app with strong identity and simple gameplay loops.

---

# 🌌 Lore
Inside the Base network, fragments of digital energy slowly began forming living creatures called BaseBlobs.

These small beings absorb energy from transactions, blocks, and network activity.

Some are sleepy.
Some are chaotic.
Some resonate with rare cosmic signals.

Nobody fully understands where they came from.
But explorers discovered one thing:

The deeper a Blob travels into the network...
The stranger it becomes.

---

# 🎯 Core Gameplay Loop

Expeditions → Cubes → Cards → Progression → New Blobs

Players:
- send blobs on expeditions
- collect Cubes
- level up blobs
- unlock cards and cosmetics
- summon new blobs
- expand their collection

# 👾 Personality System

## 😊 Happy
- Bonus XP gain
- Bright and energetic animations

## 😴 Sleepy
- Faster energy regeneration
- Relaxed sleepy animations

## 🍀 Lucky
- Slightly higher rare reward chance
- Lucky particles and effects

## ⚡ Chaotic
- Random bonus events during expeditions
- Unstable glitchy behavior

## 🌌 Cosmic
- Better rewards in late-game expeditions
- Space/cosmic visual effects
